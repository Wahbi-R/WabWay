The combined "who owes who" rollup shown at the top of Money/Settle Up — pulls together receipt splits and cash distributions. Shows "You're all settled up" when both lists are empty (the Money-screen empty state).

```jsx
<BalanceSummaryCard youOwe={[{name:'Alex Kim', amount:'3,200'}]} youAreOwed={[{name:'Josh Park', amount:'5,000'}]} />
```
